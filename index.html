About the System

The Water Leakage Reporting System is a smart platform that allows citizens to report water leakages using their mobile or computer. Reports are sent directly to authorities for quick inspection and repair.

Objectives

  • Reduce water wastage.
  • Improve response time.
  • Enable real-time monitoring.
  • Increase public participation.
